Haztech

Advanced IoT and Mobile-Based Hazard Detection

Haztech provides cutting-edge hazard detection and notification solutions through a network of sensors. Our hardware detects fire, flood, flammable gases, temperature fluctuations, and motion, transmitting data to a mobile application. This application uses a neural network to process the data and alert users to potential hazards. Additionally, Haztech offers live tracking functionalities, allowing users to share their live location.

PriceTag

PriceTag is a mobile app that leverages the accelerometer in your phone to accurately measure the distance you travel. Based on this distance, the app calculates a price according to the rate set by the driver. Additionally, PriceTag allows users to view their live location as they move, providing a seamless and transparent travel experience.

Experience the convenience of accurate travel distance tracking and real-time location visibility with PriceTag.

RailPay

Railway Booking System

RailPay is a railway booking system that simplifies train travel reservations. Once a user books a train, the app generates a ticket with a QR code and sends an email to the railway company indicating that a particular user has booked their train. This seamless process ensures that users have their tickets ready and the railway company is notified of new bookings in real-time.

In addition to RailPay, we also offer Logitech, a logistics booking app that carries out the same processes but for logistics services. Both apps ensure efficiency and convenience in their respective domains, making travel and logistics management straightforward and hassle-free.
